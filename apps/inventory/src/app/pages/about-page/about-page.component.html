About

Candle Company, nestled in the heart of the Pacific Northwest, is renowned for its vibrant, artistic candles that capture the unique spirit of the region. Our candles are not just sources of light, but pieces of art, each one meticulously handcrafted to reflect the stunning landscapes, diverse cultures, and the vibrant pop colors that the Pacific Northwest is known for.


Our custom candles are a sensory journey, with each color and scent carefully chosen to evoke the lush forests, majestic mountains, and serene coastlines of the Pacific Northwest. From the deep greens of our evergreen forests, the vibrant blues of our expansive skies, to the rich earth tones of our rugged coastlines, each candle is a tribute to the natural beauty of our region.


At Candle Company, we believe in the power of art to inspire and uplift. Our candles are more than just decor, they are a statement, a conversation starter. Whether you're looking for a unique gift, a centerpiece for your home, or just a way to bring a little bit of the Pacific Northwest into your space, Candle Company offers a unique, vibrant, and truly Pacific Northwest experience.
